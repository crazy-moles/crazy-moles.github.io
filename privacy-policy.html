Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 23, 2026

1. Introduction & Game Description

Welcome to Crazy Moles, a thrilling 3D Whack-A-Mole Mayhem game designed for players of all ages. This application is an Android wrapper for our web-based game hosted at https://crazy-moles.github.io/.

In Crazy Moles, players test their reflexes across multiple difficulty levels (Easy, Normal, and Insane) by tapping on various characters. Players earn points for Brown Moles (+10) and Golden Moles (+50), but must avoid Bomb Moles (-30). The game features combo streaks, power-ups, and a local high-score system to deliver a fun, engaging, and safe experience.

3. Information Collection and Use

Your privacy is our highest priority. We believe that games should be safe and secure. Therefore, we want to make our data practices perfectly clear:

  • No Personal Data Collected: Crazy Moles does NOT collect, store, share, or transmit any personal information. We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or any other identifying data.
  • No Analytics or Tracking: We do not use third-party analytics, behavioral tracking, or advertising SDKs that harvest user data.
  • Local Gameplay Data: Any game progress, such as your Best Score, is saved locally on your device using standard web storage mechanisms. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

4. Device Permissions

To ensure maximum privacy and security, Crazy Moles requires NO special device permissions to operate. Specifically, the app does not request access to your:

  • Camera or Microphone
  • Precise or Approximate Location (GPS)
  • Contacts or Phone Logs
  • External Storage or Files

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA & Google Play Families Policy)

Crazy Moles is a family-friendly game deemed highly suitable for children aged 4 and older. We strictly adhere to the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the Google Play Store's Families Policy requirements.

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or any age).
  • The game does not contain any inappropriate content, external links outside of the game environment, or malicious code.
  • If a parent or guardian believes that their child has somehow provided us with personal information (which the app is not designed to accept), please contact us immediately, and we will ensure the issue is resolved.
